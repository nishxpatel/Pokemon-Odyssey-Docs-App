About

Pokémon Odyssey is a fan-made Pokémon game that fuses Pokémon mechanics with Etrian Odyssey dungeon-crawler systems — Strata, F.O.E. overworld super-encounters, regional Etrian Variants, and a custom 18th type called Aether (replacing Fairy). This documentation is generated directly from the v4.1.1 design workbooks, with vanilla move and ability data backfilled from PokeAPI.
